About SPRINGRUNNERS
Springrunners is a platforming game where the objective is to make it through various levels without being launched from the Wall of Springs that chases the player. The game was influenced by a game called Speedrunners, a multiplayer platforming game where each player tries to get ahead of the others so that the other players go off of the screen and die. We wanted to make a game that had the same fast-platforming aspect of Speedrunners while being unique, so we decided to go for a singleplayer platformer with various new mechanics not present in Speedrunners such as a higher jump, a grapple you can use while in radius, and a piston to force the player to navigate the levels quickly.
